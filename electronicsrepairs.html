Why We Do It.

Facts tell, but our stories sell the truth: We exist for our customers. There have been nights where we’ve spent 10 hours hunched over a single logic board, tracing a microscopic fault that others said was "unfixable." We only charged for three.

Why? Because we follow Intecracy—a philosophy that teaches us to give back to Livingston. Every device we save is a victory against e-waste and a win for the local family who doesn't have to spend £500 on a new machine.

We are proud to teach the next generation that "broken" is just a temporary state. With over 600 videos on YouTube, you can watch our process and see the integrity we put into every solder joint.
